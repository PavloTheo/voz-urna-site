Profile

Voz Urna is the music and sound work of Pavlo Theodoridis, a composer and sound designer based in Malmö. His work spans experimental sound, music for media, and instrument design. Under the name Voz Urna, he develops Phone Dub, a method based on smartphone-recorded audio.
