Bio

Pavlo Theodoridis is a composer and sound designer based in Malmö. His work spans experimental sound, music for media, and instrument design. Under the name Voz Urna, he develops Phone Dub, a method based on smartphone-recorded audio.

Phone Dub

Phone Dub is a sound method using smartphone recordings as the only source material. Piano, environment, and noise are recorded and transformed into instruments, textures, and compositions.
